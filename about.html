Our Story

The Fortis Journey

2024

Founded with Purpose

Fortis Doors was born from a simple belief: that every space deserves a door worthy of what lies behind it. We opened our first workshop in Tashkent.

2024

First 500 Doors

Within our first year, we delivered over 500 premium doors to residential and commercial clients across the region, earning trust through quality.

2025

International Expansion

Our reputation for excellence opened doors — literally — to international markets. We began exporting premium doors to neighboring countries.

2025

Custom Design Division

We launched a dedicated custom design division, allowing clients to collaborate with our craftsmen on bespoke doors tailored to their exact vision.
